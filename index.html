How to delete your game account

You can delete your game account directly inside the app.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Launch the game
  2. Tap the Settings button located at the top-right corner
  3. Select Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

What will be deleted

  • Player progress
  • Inventory and purchased items
  • Analytics data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • All server-stored data associated with your account

Retention period

Your data will be permanently deleted within 15 days after the deletion request.
