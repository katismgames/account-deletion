How to delete your game account

You can delete your game account directly inside the app.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Launch the game
  2. Tap the Settings button located at the top-right corner
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

What will be deleted

  • Player progress
  • Inventory and purchased items
  • Analytics data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • All server-stored data associated with your account

Retention period

Your deletion request enters a pending state for 15 days. During this period, you may contact us to cancel the deletion request. After the 15-day grace period has passed, all associated data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
